Everything you need to know about Smart Invoice

Creating your first invoice is simple! After signing up, click "Create Invoice" from your dashboard. Our step-by-step form will guide you through entering your business details, client information, and invoice items. Once complete, click "Generate PDF" and your professional invoice will be ready to send in seconds.

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans offer advanced customization options. You can add your company logo, customize colors to match your brand, adjust layout preferences, and even create custom invoice templates. The free plan includes our professional default template which works great for most businesses.

Our WhatsApp integration powered by Twilio allows you to send invoices directly to your clients' WhatsApp numbers. Simply enter the client's phone number when creating an invoice, and they'll receive a message with a link to view and download their invoice. It's instant, convenient, and clients love it!

Smart Invoice supports multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, CAD, and AUD. You can select your preferred currency when creating each invoice, making it perfect for businesses working with international clients. All currency conversions and formatting are handled automatically.
